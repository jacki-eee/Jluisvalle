Towson University CIS Faculty Research Database

Senior year Capstone Group Project

  • Project Overview:

    My team's capstone project for the Spring 2025 semester was the development of the TU CIS Research Database, designed to support the Department of Computer and Information Sciences by creating a searchable database of faculty research. The aim of this project is to simplify the way students and faculty access research information, making it more convenient to find faculty members based on interests.

    To build this database, we researched similar systems from other institutions, developed wireframes to outline the structure and user interface, and consistently incorporated feedback from our client throughout the development process. The result is a platform that allows users to search by faculty name and area of interest. The database also includes links to faculty research pages and other relevant resources, offering a user-friendly way to find faculty work.
